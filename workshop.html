Workshops

"He who has the knowledge has the responsibility to impart it to the students." Rabindranath Tagore

Our workshops focus on building projects from scratch.

The whole purpose of workshops is twofold. One - to help people dirty their hands and try out practical electronics, and two - to unleash their creativity to the fullest amount that they possibly can.

We do not provide codes, but help students arrive at the code themselves. This builds their confidence and gives them the skill set required to build a project from scratch. We conduct workshops in the following broad categories.

  • 1. Robotics Workshop

  • 2. Android Workshop

  • 3. Embedded Workshop
